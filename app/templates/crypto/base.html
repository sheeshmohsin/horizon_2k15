Rules

  • Every level has an answer that'll take you to the next level.
  • In this game any thing can be a clue. You need an open mind with good observations.
  • All answers are lowercase characters or number 0-9. Spaces, special characters are not allowed.
  • If the answer is Hello World, then concatenate it to helloworld.
  • For hints or questions, please refer to the Facebook page
  • In case you win, you might be asked for the answers to all the levels. Please note them down somewhere.
  • The decision of the Horizon 2015 committee is final and binding in case of any disputes.
  • Larry Page and Sergey Brin devised a search engine. Try to use it extensively.
